Why It Makes Sense for Datadog
  • Building security capabilities (acquired Sqreen 2021)
  • Runtime security complements observability platform
  • 26,000+ customers as upsell opportunity
  • Competition with CrowdStrike and PANW for single platform
Deal Economics
  • $1B = ~18-20x current ARR (reasonable for high-growth)
  • Datadog market cap: ~$50B → acquisition is <2% of value
  • Precedent: Sqreen acquired for ~$80M (~10x ARR)
⚠️ Problem for Investors
$900M valuation + $180M raised = investors need ~$2.7B+ for 3x return. A $1B exit returns ~1.1x—below venture threshold.
